THE 10-POINT CHECKLIST
📈
1. TREND CONFIRMED
Only trade in the direction the stock is already moving. Uptrend = calls. Downtrend = puts. Never fight the trend.
📊
2. OPEN INTEREST 10,000+
Minimum 10,000 OI on any contract you enter. Below that = no liquidity = wide spreads = hard to exit. Auto-disqualified.
📉
3. VOLUME CONFIRMS
Volume should be moving in the same direction as price. High volume on a down day = strong bearish signal.
💰
4. LIMIT ORDER ALWAYS
Entry price = (Bid + Ask) ÷ 2. Never use a market order on options. You'll overpay on the spread every time.
🌡️
5. IV RANK UNDER 50%
High IV = expensive options. IV Rank above 50% means you're overpaying for premium. Wait for IV to come down.
📅
6. EXPIRATION WINDOW
Swing trades: 10–45 days out. LEAPs: 180+ days. Too close = time decay kills you. Too far = too expensive.
🛡️
7. MAX LOSS KNOWN
Max loss = premium paid. Know this number before you enter. Never risk more than 5% of your account on one trade.
🎯
8. PROFIT TARGET SET
Swing: take profit at 50–75% gain. LEAP: take profit at 100–200% gain. Set the alert BEFORE you enter.
💵
9. BUDGET FITS
Swing max: $500/contract. LEAP max: $2,000/contract. Never oversize a single options position.
🔄
10. BREAKEVEN REALISTIC
The stock must reach breakeven by expiry. If the move required is more than 15%, probability drops significantly.
